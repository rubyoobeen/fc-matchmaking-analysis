Finding 01

Squad strength gap predicts outcome — up to a point

Win rate by squad-grade gap · 30,000+ matches

Win rate climbs from 40% to 60% as the gap widens to 5 grade points — beyond that, sample size gets too thin to trust.

Even at the largest reliable gaps, the stronger side still loses about 4 in 10 matches.
Finding 02

A player's own grade barely matters

Individual grade, winners vs. losers

Confirmed twice — in Python and independently in Tableau — a single player's grade shows almost no link to winning. What matters is the matchup, not the number.

Finding 03

Season version is the real driver

Same player, different cards

One player's conversion rate swung up to 4x across his own cards. Premium seasons (TOTY, ICON TM, Heroes) dominate the win-rate rankings.
